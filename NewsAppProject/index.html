News Application

Goals of the app

  • To design an app to find and display news.
  • Create an app that looks good and easy to use on mobile.
  • Provide good navigation and interactional flow for users.

User Experience

Audience

  • Students: Students may use the news app to check the latest news while heading to classes or while waiting in-between classes.
  • Business Person: A business person may use the news app while taking a lunch break or while commuting to work to catch up on recent news.
  • Other: Other can refer to anyone else as many different people may use a news app for different purposes. This can include travelers, teenagers, and other people who may check the news daily.

Scenarios

Scenario 1, Audience Category:Student

Leon Lee is a 20 year old college student attending Rochester Institute of Technology. He has an interest in what's current in the news around the world and uses his phone constantly as he's always on the go. He plans to use the news app to check the news he finds interesting while he's heading to his classes and in-between classes.

Scenario 2, Audience Category:Business Person

Kellie Jensen is a 24 year old post college student living near Boston, Massachusetts. She works at a Bank of America located in the financial district in down-town Boston. Each morning she commutes over 30 minutes to get to work and uses her phone to read the news. She plans to use the news app to read the news during her morning commute to keep her occupied while on the train.

Scenario 3, Audience Category:Other

Sam Moore is a 44 year old international aid worker who travels around the world often to help those in need. He's been to places such as Afghanistan and Somalia to teach young children and enjoys what he does. Since he travels a lot he has to spend much of his time on the plane. During this time he usually reads novels but would like to use this app so that we is able to read the news as well.

Competitive Analysis

App 1: New York Times App

Pros: Ability to save news for 7 days and interface that is easy to navigate and organized.

Cons: Banner ads at the bottom of screen.

App 2: CNN App

Pros: Shows local news and weather.

Cons: Sections not organized that well.

App 3: AP Mobile

Pros: Easy to navigate and good looking interface.

Cons: Banner ads at the bottom of screen.

Topics

Allows the user to search for a key term in the articles listed

  • Start and end date selection
  • key terms search box

Functional Requirements

  • Navigation - The navigation should be intuitive and consistent throughout the design.
  • Information Access - The user should be able to easily search for a story they've heard about.
  • Favorites - The user should be able to save the articles they find interesting.
  • Share - The user will be able to share articles.
